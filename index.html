Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a trip?

Tap the + button on the My Trips tab to create a stay, transport, or day trip.

How do I contact another user?

Open a trip/listing and tap "Message" to start a conversation with the host or driver.

How do I delete my account?

You can delete your account directly in the app. Go to your Profile, type in your email, and tap "Delete Account".

How do I report a user?

You can report or block a user directly from the chat screen using the menu in the top right corner.
